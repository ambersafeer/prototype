Experience a cupful of creativity.

The online destination for student artists, designers, and more.

Are you a college student looking for a resourceful website to help boost your artistic journey? Look no further than the Creative Café, based in New Brunswick, NJ! Our aim is to provide you with a thriving community of like-minded artists as you explore our many helpful reviews, inspirational art showcases, related readings/blogs, and more. As a group of college-aged artists, our team understands the difficulties of experiencing artist block, making time to create, and finding budget-friendly art supplies. That's why here at the Creative Café, we provide countless resources to combat these issues so that you can continue on your artist journey.

Continue down to view some of the beautiful artworks here in New Brunswick, or click around to explore what else is on the menu at our cyber-café. Stay tuned to see what we brew up next!
